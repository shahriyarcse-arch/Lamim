The Problem

Scattered Tools.
Fragmented Journey.

Your spiritual life deserves better than 5 different apps that don't talk to each other.

Privacy Nightmare

Most apps require accounts, send data to servers, and track your spiritual habits. Your journey is personal — it should stay that way.

Too Many Apps

One for salah, one for dhikr, one for habits, another for finance. Your spiritual ecosystem shouldn't need 5 subscriptions.

Midnight Reset Bug

Standard calendars reset at midnight — but your Tahajjud after 1 AM gets filed as "tomorrow." Islamic days deserve Islamic logic.

The Solution

One Dashboard.
Everything Connected.

Lamim unifies your entire Islamic lifestyle into a single, beautiful, offline-first experience.

Zero Servers, Zero Tracking — Every byte stays on your device. No accounts, no cloud, no compromise.

Features

Everything You Need.
Nothing You Don't.

Six integrated modules covering every aspect of your Islamic lifestyle.

Salah Tracker

Live countdown to the next prayer using local sun-angle math. A 3-week interactive heatmap with glassmorphic visual feedback.

Dhikr Counter

Premium glowing tap button with ripple animations, haptic vibration, and streak tracking. Feels like real tasbeeh beads.

Mujahid (Habit Forge)

Track bad habits, log relapses, earn progression badges, and use guided 4-7-8 breathing exercises to combat urges.

Smart Finance Ledger

Track income and expenses across 220+ categories with monthly summaries, visual timelines, and auto-generated PDF statements. Savings vaults with progress bars.

Spiritual Health Score

A weighted algorithm that evaluates your entire spiritual state and assigns a rank — from Ghafil to Wali.

Nafl & Sunnah

Track Sunnah Muakkadah, Tahajjud, and Witr with custom 3 AM "Waking Day" boundary logic for accurate night prayer logging.

Analysis Engine

Comprehensive dashboard with PDF export. Visualizes your spiritual health across all modules with charts, trends, and actionable insights.

Auto-Backup

Non-intrusive 30-day backup reminders. Export your entire progress as a JSON file. Never lose your journey.
