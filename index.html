Features

Everything You Need.
Nothing You Don't.

Six integrated modules that cover every dimension of your spiritual and daily life.

Salah Tracker

Live countdown to the next prayer using local sun-angle math. A 3-week interactive heatmap with visual feedback for Jama'at, alone, and missed prayers.

Dhikr Counter

A premium tap counter with haptic vibration, ripple animations, and streak tracking that replicates the feel of real tasbeeh beads.

Mujahid — Habit Forge

Track habits, log relapses, earn streak badges, and use guided 4-7-8 breathing exercises to strengthen self-discipline.

Smart Finance Ledger

Track income and expenses across 220+ categories with monthly summaries, visual timelines, savings vaults, and auto-generated PDF statements.

Spiritual Health Score

A weighted algorithm evaluates your salah, dhikr, and habits to assign a spiritual rank — from Ghafil to Wali.

Nafl & Sunnah Tracker

Track Sunnah Muakkadah, Tahajjud, and Witr with intelligent 3 AM "Waking Day" boundaries for accurate night prayer logging.
