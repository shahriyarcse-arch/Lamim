Features

Everything You Need.
Nothing You Don't.

Seven integrated modules that cover every dimension of your spiritual life.

Salah Tracker

Live countdown to the next prayer with local sun-angle calculations. Interactive 3-week heatmap with visual feedback.

Dhikr Counter

A premium tap counter with haptic vibration, ripple animations, and streak tracking that feels like real tasbeeh beads.

Mujahid — Habit Forge

Track habits, log relapses, earn badges, and use guided 4-7-8 breathing exercises to strengthen self-discipline.

Finance Ledger

Track income and expenses across 220+ categories. Monthly summaries, visual timelines, savings vaults, and PDF export.

Spiritual Health Score

A weighted algorithm evaluates your salah, dhikr, and habits to assign a spiritual rank — from Ghafil to Wali.

Nafl & Sunnah Tracker

Track Sunnah Muakkadah, Tahajjud, and Witr with intelligent 3 AM “Waking Day” boundaries for accurate night prayers.
