Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Your data stays yours

Lamim does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, no email addresses, and no cloud servers. Everything you enter remains on your device's local storage and never leaves it.

No tracking

Lamim contains no analytics SDKs, no tracking pixels, no cookies, and no third-party monitoring tools. We do not — and cannot — see how you use the app.

No internet required

Lamim works fully offline. The only network requests are optional: prayer time auto-detection (via your IP's general location, with your permission) and currency exchange rates. Both can be disabled in Settings.

Third-party services

The following services may be contacted only if you explicitly enable the relevant feature:

  • ipapi.co / bigdatacloud.net — approximate location detection for automatic prayer times. No personal identifier is sent.
  • open.er-api.com — live currency exchange rates for the Finance module.

No data from Lamim is ever sent to these services beyond the bare minimum request needed for the feature to function.

Backups

You may export a backup file at any time. This file is a local download — Lamim has no mechanism to receive or store it remotely.

Changes

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the update. Because Lamim has no accounts, we cannot notify you individually — please check back if you are concerned.

If you have questions, open an issue at github.com/shahriyarcse-arch/Lamim.
